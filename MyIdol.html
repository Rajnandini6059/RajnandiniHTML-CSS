My Idol

Ratan Tata

Ratan Naval Tata (1937–2024) was a preeminent Indian industrialist and philanthropist who served as the chairman of the Tata Group for over two decades. He is widely credited with transforming the conglomerate from an India-centric business into a global powerhouse through high-profile international acquisitions and a steadfast commitment to ethical leadership.
